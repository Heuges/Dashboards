05 — Usage
Logo Usage Rules

✓ Do

  • Use on pure black or deep navy backgrounds
  • Maintain clear space equal to the shield width on all sides
  • Use the metallic gold gradient for all gold elements
  • Always include ™ symbol after "VERIFY"
  • Use horizontal wordmark for headers and email signatures
  • Use stacked wordmark for square/social formats

✗ Don't

  • Place logo on white or light colored backgrounds
  • Recolor the gold to any other color
  • Add glow, drop shadows, or blur effects
  • Stretch, skew, or rotate the logo
  • Use flat yellow as a substitute for mirror gold
  • Remove or alter the battlements/crenellations
