🔍 Analysis

Where CEM Stands

CEM revenue is above the reduced breakeven ($56K/week) in 4 of the last 5 weeks. However, it remains below the full strength breakeven ($130K/week) — meaning CEM cannot yet afford to bring back all staff.

3-Week Projection

Based on the last 3 weeks, revenue has an upward trend of +$12,906/week. Projected weeks 6–8 show revenue between $91K–$104K/week — comfortably above reduced BE but still $26K–$39K short of full strength BE. At current trajectory, CEM would need approximately 6–8 more weeks of this growth rate to reach full strength breakeven.

Path to Full Strength

To hit full strength breakeven ($130K/week), CEM needs +$52K/week more revenue (67% increase from current avg). If the +$12.9K/week trend holds, CEM could reach full strength breakeven by approximately late June / early July 2026.
